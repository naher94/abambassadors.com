  The Future of Hospitality: AI, Margins & Hotel Investment Trends
  
  In this episode of Hospitality Talks,   Abid Butt and Sam-Erik Ruttmann sit down with John Bortz, Chairman and CEO of Pebblebrook Hospitality Trust, to discuss the future of hotel ownership, operational efficiency, AI adoption, and the evolving hospitality landscape.
  
  As hotel owners continue to navigate rising labor costs, operational pressure, inflation, and changing traveler behavior, technology and AI are rapidly becoming critical tools for protecting margins and improving guest experiences.
  
  In this conversation, we explore:
  
  ✔️ How hotel owners are responding to rising operational costs
  ✔️ Why AI is becoming a competitive advantage in hospitality
  ✔️ The role of automation, robotics, and predictive maintenance
  ✔️ The recovery of urban hotels and leisure travel trends
  ✔️ Hotel investment outlook and transaction market insights
  ✔️ Why operational efficiency is now a leadership priority
  ✔️ The future impact of World Cup tourism and experiential travel
  ✔️ Advice for the next generation entering hospitality
  
  One standout insight from John:
  
  “Those who know how to use AI and are comfortable using it are the ones who are going to get ahead faster.”
  
  This episode offers valuable insights for:
  • Hotel owners and investors
  • Hospitality executives
  • Real estate professionals
  • Hotel technology leaders
  • Hospitality students and future leaders
